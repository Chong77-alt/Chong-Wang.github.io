About me

I am from Nanyang, Henan, and I am currently a master's student at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Beijing Jiaotong University. My research focuses on knowledge-driven machine learning, which includes physics-informed neural networks and neural differential equations. Additionally, I have a keen interest in diffusion models.
